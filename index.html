About Me

I am an engineer and half-musician based in Barcelona, with special interest in audio and music processing and analysis, music information retrieval (MIR), and machine learning.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Audiovisual Systems Engineering from Universitat Pompeu Fabra (2011-2015), a Specialist Diploma on Data Mining and Analysis from Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (2016), and a Master's degree in Sound and Music Computing from Universitat Pompeu Fabra (2016-2017).

I am currently (2017-) a PhD student at the Music Information Research Lab from the Music Technology Group in Universitat Pompeu Fabra, working on the computational analysis of (polyphonic) singing voice under the supervision of Dr. Emilia Gómez. Our main focus is the pitch analysis of vocal music: multiple F0 estimation and intonation analysis.
